## Keynote Speakers

### 张路加牧师

**张路加牧师** will serve as the main speaker for the CCFN 2026 Retreat.

### Dr. Charles Lee / 李勉群教授

**Dr. Charles Lee** is the Hansen Professor of Accounting at the Foster School of Business, University of Washington. He is also the Moghadam Family Professor Emeritus of Management and Accounting at the Stanford Graduate School of Business.

Dr. Lee's research explores how human cognitive limitations affect investor behavior and market pricing. His work has been widely followed and recognized, including the American Accounting Association FARS Lifetime Achievement Award and twelve national and university-level teaching awards. He has also been named an AAA Distinguished Scholar and received Stanford University's Asian American Faculty Achievement and Service Award.

From 2004 to 2008, Dr. Lee served as Managing Director at Barclays Global Investors, now BlackRock. As Global Head of Equity Research and Co-Head of North America Active Equity, he led global active equity research and helped oversee the firm's North America active equity business.

Dr. Lee and his wife, Lily, are Christians. Over the years, they have served graduate students and visiting scholars at the University of Michigan, Cornell University, UC Berkeley, Stanford University, and the University of Washington through evangelism and discipleship. They frequently speak at churches, workshops, and conferences. Dr. Lee has served on the board of Ambassadors for Christ and has spoken multiple times at Truth Forum.
